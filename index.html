Wanted to find a place to play some arcade games for my birthday. They had a decent sized arcade with about 30 games to choose from. The large Hungry Hippo game was hilarious to play. Smaller kids will need a little help to be fast with moving the handle.

Sara Wilsson

I played for about an hour and got up to 2233 points. The monkey is my favorite prize! They also have go karts in the back and mini golf out front. They also have delicious cheese fries with seasoning on top.
